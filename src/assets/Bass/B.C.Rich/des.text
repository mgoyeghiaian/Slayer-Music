
CHRIS KAEL SIGNATURE WARLOCK

Price:$1,299.99

The Chris Kael Signature Warlock 4 string bass features a mahogany body in Satin Black finish with Abalone binding on the body, fretboard and headstock. An Ebony fretboard sits atop a neck-through 5-piece Maple/Wenge neck with a Shredzilla Ultra Slim Contour and is topped with black Grover tuning machines on the Beast headstock. The Chris Kael Warlock Bass is equipped a P/J pickup configuration with a EMG Split-Coil P pickup at the neck and a EMG Single-Coil J pickup at the bridge. an active EMG EQ, bridge and neck volume controls, and treble boost/cut, bass boost/cut.
